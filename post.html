Experience

Built For This

My journey in construction and real estate began with a deeply personal endeavor: assisting in the development of our family's equestrian center in California. This hands-on experience provided me with invaluable insights into the complexities of construction and the importance of meticulous planning and execution.

Seeking to broaden my horizons and make a meaningful impact, I ventured to Ghana, Africa, where I volunteered to build a school. This experience was transformative, as it allowed me to witness firsthand the profound difference that well-constructed infrastructure can make in improving the lives of others.

Now, as a project engineer at McCarthy Building Companies, I have the opportunity to apply the skills and knowledge gained from my diverse experiences to oversee and manage construction projects with precision and dedication. Each project presents new challenges and opportunities to make a positive impact in the built environment and the communities it serves.

My journey in construction and real estate has been characterized by a commitment to excellence, a passion for innovation, and a dedication to making a difference in every project I undertake.
